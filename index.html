Bell House Restaurant, one of the finest places to have lunch and dinner in Shelbyville.

We are in the heart of Downtown Shelbyville in a home that was fully renovated from 2007-2009 by Bobby and Sue Andriot. If you visit our "About" page, you can read about the progress that this house has been through in the past century.

We hope that as you enter Bell House, you feel welcomed.

We are striving to be fine dining, but still delight in the "down home hospitality" that makes you feel welcome.

There is no particular dress code; We will take you as you are.

Reservations are not necessary, but they are accepted and appreciated.

We hope your experience in Bell House is one that you desire to have again and to tell people about. Good Service, Good Food, and a Beautiful Environment...you can't go wrong! We look forward to serving you!

We offer a wide variety of services, so if you don't see what you're looking for on the list, please don't hesitate to ask. 502-437-5678
