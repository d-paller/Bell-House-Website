Address and Directions

Address: 721 Main Street Shelbyville, KY 40065

From Louisville:

Scenic Route:

Take US60 East. You will go through Middletown, Eastwood, Simpsonville, and then you will enter the lovely town of Shelbyville. Continue going straight. You will come to a horse statue in the middle of the road that splits US60. This one way street that you are on has become Main Street. Continue straight. You will be passing many beautiful homes, and some businesses....be looking on the left for a pink house. Once you see the pink house, you have arrived! You can park on the street in the front or in the parking lot around the back.

Faster Route:

64 East toward Shelbyville. Get off at exit 32B. Follow the road straight until you come to a large Intersection with a Walgreens on your right. You will want to be in the right lane and turn right onto US60 East. Stay Straight until you come to a horse statue in the middle of the road that splits US60. This one way street that you are on has become Main Street. Continue straight. You will be passing many beautiful homes, and some business....be looking on the left for a pink house. Once you see the pink house, you have arrived! You can park on the street in the front or in the parking lot around the back.

From Lexington:

Take 64 West. Get off at Exit 35 Shelbyville. Take a right off the exit onto Highway 53. Continue straight until you come to a big intersection next to the McDonalds. Take a left at the light onto US60 West which becomes Washington Street. The back Parking lot is on the left passed the 7th Street Stop Light!
